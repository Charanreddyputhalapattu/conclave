PROJECT SHOWCASE

Project showcase by individuals or startups where they can present their innovative humanitarian technologies by making a stall in the front auditorium. Could be incubated or awarded.

HUMANITARIAN VIDEO CONTEST

A Humanitarian Video Competition where students or any individuals can make a 3 min video of a real-time Everlong problem statement in their locality and sustainable solution. The top videos could be presented on the day and could be supported with some grant or award.
